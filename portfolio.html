ABOUT

I'm a Visual Designer and Front-End Developer. I enjoy illustrating vector pattern designs and building web projects that are aesthetically appealing, fully functional, and mobile-first responsive. I make it a habit to stay up-to-date on current design best practices. This year, I started specializing in surface pattern designs for Print-On-Demand products such as T-shirts, tote bags, leggings, notebooks, etc. in order to launch my own POD online business. Creating uplifting designs is my way of adding value into others lives. Starting in January 2020, a portion of T-shirt sales will go to mental health organizations.
